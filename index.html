Our Mission

Park Lane Conceirge is one of the largest private companies in Kuwait, with a focus on the acquisition and ongoing management of property, private jet charter and real estate.
Park Lane Conceirge's brands include high profile communities.
Our first mission is to be as flexible as possible to serve you; whether you need a private jet for business or pleasure, our expert team is available to listen to your needs and provide the perfect flight solution.
We understand that your time is precious. Hiring a private jet gives you the ability to travel at your preferred time, to and from the most convenient airports, with control over every element of your journey.
Park Lane Conceirge's offers a unique perspective on how a profitable business should operate. Our due diligence process includes not only a physical inspection of the property, but also a monetary and marketing assessment to identify opportunities for improvement. Competitive products in the marketplace are analysed along with economic factors to provide the most detailed report possible to offer better rental solutions.
We want to make accommodation affordable and tailored to individual needs, which vary greatly between personalities. We want to cut out the middleman, the agent, who often becomes the one who decides where to stay instead of meeting your tastes.
